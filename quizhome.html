WEB DESIGNING QUIZ

Why should you become web developer

these are the 8 reasons to consider

  1. UNLOCK YOUR CREATIVITY
    • Learning to code is a lot like learning an instrument or a language. Once you understand the foundation, you can flex your creative muscles
  2. YOU’LL BE PART OF A COMMUNITY
    • The San Diego tech scene and online community for developers are huge. Take advantage of the technical support and like-minded people available to help.
  3. YOU DON’T NEED A DEGREE
    • The necessity of a formal degree in web development has decreased with the rise in coding bootcamps similar to LEARN academy who can teach all the skills needed for a career and offer job placement services.
  4. THE PAY IS GOOD
    • The median salary for a web software developer in San Diego, CA (according to Salary.com) is $83,443, putting a web-developer on the higher end of starting salaries across many different fields.
  5. JOB FLEXIBILITY
    • Being a web developer gives you the flexibility to work freelance or part of an org
  6. THE FUTURE LOOKS GOOD
    • The dependence on technology and mobile devices show no sign of stopping. The Bureau of Labor Statistics reports the job outlook from 2016 – 2026 will increase by 15% over that time span.
  7. SPECIALIZATION
    • LEARN academy will teach you the coding languages you need to be successful out the door, but there are 500+ languages to learn, each having their own role. This gives you the ability to continually expand your knowledge.
  8. CODING CAN BE FUN
    • Web development continues to be one of the more exciting careers available by allowing people to work in just about any industry, including smaller companies and start-ups
